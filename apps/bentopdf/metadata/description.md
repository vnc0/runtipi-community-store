BentoPDF is a powerful, privacy-first PDF toolkit that runs entirely in your browser. All PDF processing happens client-side, ensuring your files never leave your device.

**Key Features:**
- Merge, split, and rotate PDFs
- Compress PDFs to reduce file size
- Add annotations, signatures, and watermarks
- Fill and flatten PDF forms
- Convert documents (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) to PDF
- Convert images (JPG, PNG, WEBP, SVG, etc.) to PDF
- Convert ebooks (EPUB, MOBI, CBZ) to PDF
- Extract text and images from PDFs
- Protect PDFs with passwords
- No server-side processing - complete privacy
